Combine in your rice cooker:

Ingredients
2 cups Basmati Rice
3 cups water
3 teaspoons salt
2 cloves
2 cardamom pods
One pinch cinnamon
3 Tbsp vegetable oil
1/2 tsp each mustard, cumin, chile
2 tsp onion powder
3 chopped garlic cloves
1/4 teaspoon turmeric
Adapted for rice cooker from Simply Recipes.

Directions
Combine all ingredients save the cloves in the rice cooker. Cook on "white rice" setting (15 minutes).
