--- title: Easy Indian Rice permalink: /recipes/easy-indian-rice/index.html layout: recipe id: recipes ingredients: - 2 cups Basmati Rice - 3 cups water - 3 teaspoons salt - 2 cloves - 2 cardamom pods - One pinch cinnamon - 3 Tbsp vegetable oil - 1/2 tsp each mustard, cumin, chile - 2 tsp onion powder - 3 chopped garlic cloves - 1/4 teaspoon turmeric forward: 'Adapted for rice cooker from Simply Recipes.' --- Combine all ingredients save the cloves in the rice cooker. Cook on "white rice" setting (15 minutes). Add cloves at end.
