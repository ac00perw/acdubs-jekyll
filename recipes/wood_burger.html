--- title: Hamburger by Woodburgers permalink: /recipes/burger/index.html layout: recipe id: recipes keywords: ["American", "beef", "burger"] ingredients: - 2 pounds 80/20 ground beef - 1/2 small onion, finely chopped - 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce - 2 teaspoons soy sauce - 1 teaspoon garlic powder - Freshly ground pepper forward: 'This is my favorite burger recipe. It comes from a few different sources. Calling it Woodburgers makes it sound much drier than it actually is!' --- Combine all ingredients. Top with Cabot American cheese to make it more amazinger. Seriously sharp works too.
